Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management

The Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management (BSTM) program is designed to prepare students for leadership roles in the tourism industry. The program covers a range of topics including tourism marketing, event planning, travel and tour operations, hospitality management, and sustainable tourism development. Students will also gain practical experience through internships and exposure to the tourism industry.

Core Subjects:

  • Tourism Marketing
  • Event Planning and Management
  • Tour Operations and Management
  • Hospitality Management
  • Sustainable Tourism Development
  • Tourism Law and Ethics
  • Global Tourism Trends

Career Opportunities:

  • Tourism Manager
  • Event Planner
  • Travel Agent
  • Tour Guide
  • Hospitality Manager
  • Tourism Marketing Consultant

Program Highlights:

The BSTM program provides students with a strong foundation in tourism management, enhanced by real-world industry experiences through internships, site visits, and workshops with tourism professionals.
