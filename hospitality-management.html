Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management

The Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM) program is designed to prepare students for a career in the hospitality industry, focusing on the management of hotels, resorts, restaurants, and other service-oriented businesses. The program covers key areas such as customer service, hotel operations, event planning, food and beverage management, and marketing, all while emphasizing leadership and organizational skills.

Core Subjects:

  • Hotel and Restaurant Management
  • Event Planning and Management
  • Food and Beverage Service
  • Hospitality Marketing
  • Accounting for Hospitality Industry
  • Tourism Management
  • Human Resources in Hospitality

Career Opportunities:

  • Hotel Manager
  • Restaurant Manager
  • Event Planner
  • Food and Beverage Director
  • Hospitality Consultant
  • Tourism Officer

Program Highlights:

The BSHM program emphasizes practical experience, with students participating in internships and hands-on training in hospitality management. This provides students with real-world exposure to the challenges and opportunities within the hospitality industry.
