Bachelor of Science in Community Management

The Bachelor of Science in Community Management (BSCM) program prepares students to address the social, economic, and environmental challenges of communities. The program focuses on community development, social work, public health, and sustainable community practices. Students will develop skills in leadership, project management, and advocacy to empower communities and foster positive social change.

Core Subjects:

  • Community Development and Planning
  • Social Work and Advocacy
  • Public Health and Community Wellbeing
  • Leadership and Management
  • Project Management for Community Development
  • Environmental Sustainability
  • Community Research and Assessment

Career Opportunities:

  • Community Manager
  • Social Worker
  • Project Coordinator
  • Public Health Administrator
  • NGO Program Manager
  • Community Outreach Specialist

Program Highlights:

The BSCM program emphasizes practical skills through community-based projects, internships, and workshops that provide hands-on experience in the field of community management and development.
